Alex Michaelides

British-Cypriot Psychological Thriller Born 1977

Alex Michaelides is a bestselling author known for his gripping psychological thrillers. Born in Cyprus to a Greek-Cypriot father and English mother, he studied English literature at Cambridge University and psychotherapy at Harvard.

Before becoming a novelist, Michaelides worked as a screenwriter, and his experience in film storytelling is evident in his tightly plotted, cinematic novels.
