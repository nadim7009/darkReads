The Silent Patient

by Alex Michaelides

★★★★☆ 4.2 (12,345 ratings)
Thriller Mystery Psychological

Synopsis

Alicia Berenson's life seems perfect. A renowned painter, a beautiful house, and a loving husband. But everything shatters when she shoots her husband five times in the face and never speaks again.

The mystery captures the world's attention as Alicia's art skyrockets in value. She is hidden away at the Grove, a forensic unit, as a psychotherapist tries to get her to talk.
